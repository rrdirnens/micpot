Cookie Policy

This page explains what cookies are and how we use them on this website.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. They help websites remember information about your visit and support basic functionality.

2. How we use cookies

We use cookies to store your cookie-consent choice and to support essential website functionality (for example, navigation and language preference).

3. Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies using your browser settings. If you disable cookies, some parts of the website may not work correctly.

4. Updates

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on this page.
